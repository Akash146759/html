Using Float Properties

In this example, the images will float to the left and right of the paragraph text, with proper margins and spacing to ensure the layout remains visually appealing.

This text demonstrates how the `float` property works. The image on the right is floated to the right, while the image on the left is floated to the left. Text wraps neatly around the images, and margins ensure that the text does not overlap with the images. The float property is especially useful for creating attractive layouts in combination with inline images or other content. Notice how the flow of the content adjusts to the placement of the images.
