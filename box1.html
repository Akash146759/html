This is an example of a box model at work! Notice the layers:

  • Content: The text and elements inside the box.
  • Padding: Space between the content and the border (in purple).
  • Border: The solid or dashed line around the padding (in blue).
  • Margin: Space outside the border that separates it from other elements.

The colors and borders show the different layers of the box model. Try changing the border style, padding, and margin to experiment!
